💡 What
Added `:focus-visible` pseudo-class to the hidden `.lantern` link styles in `assets/css/shared.v2.css`, added descriptive `title` attributes to the `.lantern` links in `desktop.html` and `mobile.html`, and added a descriptive `title` attribute to the YouTube `iframe` in `pages/story.html`.

🎯 Why
To improve keyboard navigation visibility for visually impaired or keyboard-only users who tab to hidden portals, and to provide necessary dual-labeling (aria-label + title) and screen reader support for icon-only links and embedded content.

📸 Before/After
Before: The hidden `.lantern` portal remained mostly invisible (`opacity: 0.07`) when focused via keyboard, icon-only links lacked native tooltips, and the YouTube iframe lacked a descriptive title.
After: The hidden `.lantern` portal becomes clearly visible (`opacity: 0.65`) when focused via keyboard, icon-only links have native tooltips, and the YouTube iframe provides context for screen readers.

♿ Accessibility
- Ensured keyboard focus visibility (`:focus-visible`) for hidden portals.
- Implemented dual-labeling for icon-only interactive elements by adding `title` attributes.
- Ensured embedded `iframe` content is accessible to screen readers by providing descriptive `title` attributes.
